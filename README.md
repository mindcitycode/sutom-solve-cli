# Usage

node ./src/index.js m . /lt /i . . . . . . . + lx

=> [ 'merveilleux', 'monoclinaux' ]

 means word must match regexp ^m.[^lt][^i].......$ and include letters l and x anywhere where letter in not specified (negation groups or any char)
